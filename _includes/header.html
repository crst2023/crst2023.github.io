CSE Research Symposium

The IITB CSE Research Symposium will be a two-day event (March 25 - 26, 2023) showcasing cutting-edge research done at the Department of CSE. The event will also have talks by distinguished speakers from India on three tracks: Systems, AI/ML, and Theoretical CS. The symposium will feature a poster session featuring research done by MS/Ph.D. students followed by a presentation session where a selected few will present their research to the audience. This would provide a unique opportunity for our students to interact with research scholars from other institutes and various researchers.

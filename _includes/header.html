IITB CSE Research Symposium

March 25-26, 2023
IIT Bombay, Mumbai, India


The Department of CSE at IITB is excited to organize a two-day research symposium, taking place on March 25-26, 2023. The symposium will showcase the cutting-edge research work that is being done at the Department of CSE.

The event will also have invited talks by distinguished speakers on three different tracks: Systems, AI/ML, and Theoretical CS. In addition to the talks, we will also be hosting a poster session featuring the research work done by our MS/PhD students. The symposium would provide an excellent opportunity for participants to learn about the latest advancements in computer science research and to also get a closer look at the research work culture at CSE@IITB.

Registration is open to pre-final and final year UG students and masters students from computer science and related fields.

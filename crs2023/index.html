IITB CSE Research Symposium

March 25-26, 2023
IIT Bombay, Mumbai, India


The Department of CSE at IITB is excited to organize a two-day research symposium, taking place on March 25-26, 2023. The symposium will showcase the cutting-edge research work that is being done at the Department of CSE.

The event will also have invited talks from some of our distinguished alumni on three different tracks: computer systems, AI/ML, and theoretical CS. In addition to the talks, we will also be hosting some interactive sessions, including a poster session featuring the research work done by our MS/PhD students. The symposium would provide an excellent opportunity for participants to learn about the latest advancements in computer science research and to also get a closer look at the research culture at CSE@IITB.

We invite pre-final and final year UG students and masters students from computer science and related fields to participate. Selected participants will be provided with accommodation on the IITB campus during the event. They may also be provided with a limited travel support.
